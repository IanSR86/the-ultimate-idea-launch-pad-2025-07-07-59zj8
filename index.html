Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does the 3-day launch process work?

Our streamlined process is divided into three phases: Day 1 focuses on strategy and planning, Day 2 on brand development and setup, and Day 3 on launch preparation and execution. Each day includes detailed checklists, templates, and expert guidance to ensure success.

What's included in the complete launch package?

You'll receive access to our full suite of tools including business planning templates, branding tools, legal document generators, marketing materials, website templates, and launch checklists. Plus, unlimited access to our expert support team throughout your launch.

What kind of support is available during and after launch?

We provide 24/7 email support, daily live chat hours, and scheduled 1-on-1 consultations with industry experts. Post-launch, you'll have 30 days of priority support to ensure your business gets off to a strong start.

Can I customize the templates and tools for my specific industry?

Yes! Our AI-powered customization engine adapts all materials to your specific industry and business model. You can further customize every template and tool to match your unique vision and requirements.

What happens if I need more than 3 days to launch?

While our system is optimized for a 3-day launch, you can proceed at your own pace. Your access to all tools and support remains active for 90 days, giving you plenty of time to perfect every aspect of your launch.
